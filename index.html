MEET THE SPONSORS OF PLAYER'S CHOICE!

VAIIYA

We. As One

VAIIYA is a sponsor in THE FINALS, introduced in Season 2 as the official sponsor for that season. They are experts in cybersecurity. VAIIYA is led by CEO Sal Scoria. After the CNS take-over, the showrunners brought in VAIIYA to stabilize the game show. VAIIYA's slogan is "Defending your digital frontier." and "We. As One."

HOLTOW

Digitally Insured - Virtually Secured

Holtow is one of the various sponsors of THE FINALS. In the Season 1, they were introduced as the official sponsors of the season and Las Vegas arena. Through cosmetics descriptions and in-game dialogue, it is revealed that HOLTOW is an insurance company, focusing on protecting digital assets. They are associated with slogans such as "With HOLTOW, you can risk it all" and "Digitally insured, virtually secured."

ISEUL-T

Be Anybody. Be Everybody. Be ISEUL-T.

Iseul-t is a Korean clothing line led by Yun Beom-seok (CEO). It was one of two official sponsors for Season 0 and claims to be the company responsible for creating virtual copies of the fashion trends in the real world. The brand is known for its signature cyan color in its cosmetics and advertisements in the virtual arenas promoting the “real and digital clothing” line. Iseul-t's slogan is “The Bias Wrecker” “Transcending Realities.” and "be ANYBODY be EVERYBODY be ISEUL-T"

MEET OUR CEOS

Patrick Soderlund

Embark CEO

is a Swedish businessman and video game executive. He was executive vice president in charge of Worldwide Studios at Electronic Arts (EA), overseeing all game production. He was later Chief Design Officer of EA. Söderlund was also known for his tenure as CEO of DICE, a Swedish video game development company that created the Battlefield series of games, which was acquired by EA in 2006. As of August 2016, he is reported to own approximately US$11 million of EA shares. Söderlund announced he would be leaving EA after twelve years of service on 14 August 2018. Söderlund left EA following a disappointing release for Battlefield 5. In November 2018, Söderlund was announced as the CEO of Embark Studios.
